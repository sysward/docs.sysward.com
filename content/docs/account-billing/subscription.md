The **Subscription** page shows your current plan and lets you order agent
capacity. SysWard is priced per agent — **$1.00 / agent / month**. You'll see:

- **Current Subscription** — how many agents your plan covers.
- **Pricing** — the per-agent monthly price.
- **Agents to order** — enter how many agents you want, then click
  **Subscribe to _N_ agents** to change your capacity.

Use **Manage Account** to sign in to the billing portal, where you can view
invoices, update payment details, or change your plan.

## Managing billing

<a href="/images/subscription_manage_account.png" target=_blank><img src="/images/subscription_manage_account.png" alt="Manage Account button" class="img-fluid rounded"/></a>

Click **Manage Account** to open the billing portal.

<a href="/images/subscription_manage.png" target=_blank><img src="/images/subscription_manage.png" alt="Sign in to the billing portal" class="img-fluid rounded"/></a>

Enter your email to sign in to the billing portal. You'll be emailed a one-time
passcode (OTP).

<a href="/images/subscription_otp.png" target=_blank><img src="/images/subscription_otp.png" alt="Enter one-time passcode" class="img-fluid rounded"/></a>

Enter the one-time passcode sent to your email.

<a href="/images/subscription_upgrading.png" target=_blank><img src="/images/subscription_upgrading.png" alt="Confirm plan change" class="img-fluid rounded"/></a>

Confirm the plan you want to change to.

<a href="/images/subscription_account_details.png" target=_blank><img src="/images/subscription_account_details.png" alt="Enter billing information" class="img-fluid rounded"/></a>

Enter your billing information. Make sure the email matches the organization
owner's email — if you need a separate billing email, submit a support ticket.

<a href="/images/subscription_confirm.png" target=_blank><img src="/images/subscription_confirm.png" alt="Confirm and enter payment information" class="img-fluid rounded"/></a>

Confirm and enter your payment information.
