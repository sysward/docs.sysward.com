Your **Profile** page manages your personal account settings.

## Change password

Enter your current password and a new password, then click **Save password**.

## User settings

- **Receive email alerts** — toggle whether SysWard emails you notifications.
- **Two-factor authentication** — click **Enable** to set up 2FA. Once enabled,
  you'll enter an MFA token each time you sign in.

## API settings

Your account's **API Key** is shown here. Use **Copy** to copy it.
