## Organization settings

The **Organization Settings** page controls organization-wide options:

- **Organization name** — the name shown across the app and in your browser tab.
- **Webhook URL** — a Slack or Discord webhook that SysWard posts notifications
  to.
- **Agent dead timeout** — how long a host can go without checking in before
  it's counted as a *dead* agent on the [dashboard](/docs/using-sysward/dashboard/).

Click **Save** to apply your changes.

## Users

The **Users** table lists everyone in your organization with their ID, email,
timezone, and last login. Use the filter box to find a specific user.

### Invite a user

In the **Invite User** section, enter an **Email address** and click **Invite**
to send an invitation to join your organization.
