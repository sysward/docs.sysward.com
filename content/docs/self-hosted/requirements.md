## System requirements

* Operating System: Ubuntu 20 LTS or 22 LTS or 24 LTS
* Internet connectivity to talk to the license server
  * We do not support air gapped environments at this time

### Minimum

* CPU: 1 Cores
* Memory: 4GB RAM
* Disk: 70GB

### Recommended

* CPU: 2 Cores
* Memory: 8GB RAM
* Disk: 80GB
