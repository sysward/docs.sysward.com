## Trials

### How do I get the appliance?

Sign in to the appliance manager at
[https://appliance.sysward.com/](https://appliance.sysward.com/).

### How many agents does a trial get?

Trials support up to **10 agents**. Need more? Reach out to support.

### How long do trials last?

Trials last **14 days** from creation and can be extended by reaching out to
support.

## Billing & licensing

### How is billing handled?

We use Chargebee to manage billing, which lets you pay with either Stripe or
PayPal.

### How do I pay for a license?

In the appliance manager, click **View** on your license, then **Subscribe**.
The license state changes from `pending` to `active` once payment has been
processed.

### How do I manage my billing information or cancel?

Sign in at [https://appliance.sysward.com/](https://appliance.sysward.com/) and
click **Billing Management** in the navigation header. You can update your
information or cancel from there.

### I would prefer invoices — do you offer Net 30?

For larger customers we can generate invoices with net terms — contact support.
